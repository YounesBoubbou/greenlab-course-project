 Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving efficiency, productivity, and accuracy in various sectors. In manufacturing, AI-powered robots have automated repetitive tasks and enhanced the production process. In healthcare, AI technologies such as machine learning algorithms help diagnose diseases faster and more accurately. In finance, AI is used for risk assessment and fraud detection. Moreover, AI chatbots provide customer service support 24/7 in industries like retail and hospitality. Overall, AI has significantly optimized business processes, reduced costs, and enhanced the customer experience.
