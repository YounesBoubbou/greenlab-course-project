## Capitals of European Countries:

**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **United Kingdom:** London
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam
* **Belgium:** Brussels

**Central & Eastern Europe:**

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Slovenia:** Ljubljana

**Southern Europe:**

* **Greece:** Athens
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Portugal:** Lisbon

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Ukraine:** Kyiv
* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Lithuania:** Vilnius
* **Latvia:** Riga
* **Estonia:** Tallinn

**Other Territories:**

* **Vatican City:** Vatican City (has no formal capital as it is a city-state)
* **Monaco:** Monaco (has no formal capital as it is a principality)

**Note:** This list includes only recognized independent states in Europe. It does not include territories that are partially or fully under the control of other states.
