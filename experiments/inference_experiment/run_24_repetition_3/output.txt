 Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving productivity, enhancing decision-making capabilities, and enabling new business models. In manufacturing, AI is used for predictive maintenance, quality control, and supply chain optimization. The healthcare industry benefits from AI's ability to analyze medical data and assist in diagnosis. Finance sees AI in risk assessment, fraud detection, and customer service. Retail uses AI for personalized recommendations and inventory management. AI's impact on industries continues to grow, with new applications emerging as technology advances.
