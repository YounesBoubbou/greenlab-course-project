 The article highlights the progress made in adopting renewable energy sources like solar and wind power due to technological advancements and decreasing costs. However, their intermittency poses challenges for energy systems that require the development of energy storage technologies and grid integration strategies. Policymakers have implemented incentives such as tax credits and feed-in tariffs to accelerate the transition. To achieve carbon neutrality, a holistic approach is needed, including energy efficiency, sustainable infrastructure development, and international collaboration.
