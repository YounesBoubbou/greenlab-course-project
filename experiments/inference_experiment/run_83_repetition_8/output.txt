This paper provides an overview of artificial intelligence (AI), encompassing its definition, applications, subfields, history, and societal impact. 

Key insights include: AI's broad scope, from everyday applications like search engines to complex tasks like autonomous vehicles; the evolution of AI research goals, driven by advancements in techniques like deep learning; and the significant funding surge after 2012, fueled by breakthroughs in AI capabilities.

The paper also highlights the growing concerns surrounding AI's unintended consequences and potential risks, emphasizing the need for regulatory policies to ensure responsible development and deployment.
