## European Capital Cities:

**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris
* **United Kingdom:** London
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Switzerland:** Bern

**Central & Eastern Europe:**

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava

**Southern Europe:**

* **Italy:** Rome
* **Greece:** Athens
* **Portugal:** Lisbon
* **Malta:** Valletta

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm
* **Belgium:** Brussels

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Ukraine:** Kyiv
* **Moldova:** Chisinau
* **Estonia:** Tallinn
* **Latvia:** Riga
* **Lithuania:** Vilnius

**Additional Notes:**

* Monaco and Vatican City are not recognized as independent countries and do not have official capitals.
* While Lisbon is the capital of Portugal, the historical center is known as Alfama.
