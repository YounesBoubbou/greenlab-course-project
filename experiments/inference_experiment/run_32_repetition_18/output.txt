 Here is a list of the capital cities of most European countries, although not all European countries have a capital city within their borders (e.g., San Marino and Vatican City):

1. Albania: Tirana
2. Andorra: Andorra la Vella
3. Austria: Vienna (Wien)
4. Belarus: Minsk
5. Belgium: Brussels (Brussel)
6. Bosnia and Herzegovina: Sarajevo (Sarajevo) (The entities within the country have different capitals: Sarajevo for the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Mostar for the Republika Srpska, and Banja Luka for the Brcko District.)
7. Bulgaria: Sofia
8. Croatia: Zagreb
9. Cyprus: Nicosia (Lefkosia) (The northern part of Nicosia is the de facto capital of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, which is recognized only by Turkey.)
10. Czech Republic: Prague (Praha)
11. Denmark: Copenhagen (København)
12. Estonia: Tallinn
13. Finland: Helsinki (Helsingfors)
14. France: Paris
15. Georgia: Tbilisi
16. Germany: Berlin
17. Greece: Athens (Athina)
18. Hungary: Budapest
19. Iceland: Reykjavik (Reykjavíkurbyrð)
20. Ireland: Dublin
21. Italy: Rome (Roma)
22. Kazakhstan (partially in Europe, mostly in Asia): Astana (Nur-Sultan)
23. Latvia: Riga
24. Liechtenstein: Vaduz
25. Lithuania: Vilnius
26. Luxembourg: Luxembourg City (Luxembourg-Stadt)
27. Malta: Valletta
28. Moldova: Chișinău
29. Monaco: Monaco (Monaco)
30. Montenegro: Podgorica
31. Netherlands: Amsterdam
32. North Macedonia: Skopje
33. Norway: Oslo
34. Poland: Warsaw (Warszawa)
35. Portugal: Lisbon (Lisboa)
36. Romania: Bucharest (București)
37. Russia (partially in Europe, mostly in Asia): Moscow (Moskva)
38. San Marino: City of San Marino (Città di San Marino)
39. Serbia: Belgrade (Beograd)
40. Slovakia: Bratislava
41. Slovenia: Ljubljana
42. Spain: Madrid
43. Sweden: Stockholm
44. Switzerland: Bern (Berne)
45. Ukraine: Kyiv (Kiev)
46. United Kingdom: London
47. Vatican City: Vatican City (Città del Vaticano)
