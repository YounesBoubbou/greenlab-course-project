 Artificial intelligence has transformed industries by improving productivity, enhancing decision-making capabilities, and automating repetitive tasks. In manufacturing, AI is used for predictive maintenance and quality control. In healthcare, it aids in diagnosis and patient care. Retail sectors benefit from personalized customer experiences and inventory management. The finance industry uses AI for risk assessment and fraud detection. These are just a few examples of how AI has revolutionized various industries by streamlining processes, reducing errors, and increasing efficiency.
