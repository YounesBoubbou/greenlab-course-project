## Capitals of European Countries:

**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris
* **United Kingdom:** London
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam

**Southern Europe:**

* **Italy:** Rome
* **Greece:** Athens
* **Portugal:** Lisbon
* **Malta:** Valletta

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Romania:** Bucharest

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm

**Central Europe:**

* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Slovenia:** Ljubljana

**Balkan & Mediterranean:**

* **Turkey:** Ankara
* **Montenegro:** Podgorica
* **Albania:** Tirana
* **Malta:** Valletta

**Note:** 
* Vatican City is not considered a country, but its capital is Vatican City.
* Monaco and San Marino are microstates within Italy and are not considered separate countries.
