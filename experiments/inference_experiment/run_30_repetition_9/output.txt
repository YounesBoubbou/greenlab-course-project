 Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving efficiency, accuracy, and innovation. In manufacturing, AI enables predictive maintenance and optimizes production lines. In healthcare, it aids in diagnostics, drug discovery, and personalized treatment plans. For finance, AI powers risk management, fraud detection, and investment strategies. In retail, AI offers personalized customer experiences and intelligent inventory management. By automating repetitive tasks, AI allows professionals to focus on creative problem-solving and strategic decision-making, driving growth and profitability in modern businesses.
