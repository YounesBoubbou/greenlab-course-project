 Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving efficiency, productivity, and innovation in various sectors. In manufacturing, AI optimizes production lines through predictive maintenance and quality control. In healthcare, it aids in diagnosing diseases faster and personalizing treatment plans. In finance, AI algorithms analyze vast amounts of data to detect fraud, manage risk, and automate investment strategies. Furthermore, AI enhances customer service in retail by powering chatbots that provide instant support 24/7. Its impact is significant, shaping the future of work, economy, and society at large.
