 Here is a list of the capitals for most European countries. Please note that Europe has several microstates whose capitals may not be immediately recognizable:

1. Albania - Tirana
2. Andorra - Andorra la Vella
3. Armenia - Yerevan
4. Austria - Vienna (Wien)
5. Azerbaijan - Baku
6. Belarus - Minsk
7. Belgium - Brussels (Brussel, Bruxelles in Dutch and French)
8. Bosnia and Herzegovina - Sarajevo (Federation of BiH), Banja Luka (Republika Srpska) and Mostar (Cantonal capital of Herzegovina-Neretva Canton)
9. Croatia - Zagreb
10. Czech Republic - Prague (Praha)
11. Denmark - Copenhagen (København)
12. Estonia - Tallinn
13. Finland - Helsinki (Helsingfors)
14. France - Paris
15. Georgia - Tbilisi
16. Germany - Berlin
17. Greece - Athens (Athina)
18. Hungary - Budapest
19. Iceland - Reykjavik
20. Ireland - Dublin
21. Italy - Rome (Roma)
22. Kosovo - Pristina (Prishtinë) (recognized as capital by Kosovo, not all other countries)
23. Latvia - Riga
24. Liechtenstein - Vaduz
25. Lithuania - Vilnius
26. Luxembourg - City of Luxembourg (Ville de Luxembourg)
27. North Macedonia - Skopje (Skoplje)
28. Malta - Valletta
29. Moldova - Chișinău
30. Monaco - Monte Carlo
31. Montenegro - Podgorica
32. Netherlands - Amsterdam
33. Norway - Oslo
34. Poland - Warsaw (Warszawa)
35. Portugal - Lisbon (Lisboa)
36. Romania - Bucharest (București)
37. Russia - Moscow (Moskva)
38. San Marino - City of San Marino (Città di San Marino)
39. Serbia - Belgrade (Beograd)
40. Slovakia - Bratislava
41. Slovenia - Ljubljana
42. Spain - Madrid
43. Sweden - Stockholm
44. Switzerland - Bern
45. Ukraine - Kyiv (Kiev in some English sources)
46. United Kingdom - London
47. Vatican City - Vatican City (Città del Vaticano)
