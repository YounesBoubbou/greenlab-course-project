 Here is a list of capital cities for most European countries:

1. Austria - Vienna (Wien)
2. Belgium - Brussels (Brussel)
3. Bosnia and Herzegovina - Sarajevo
4. Bulgaria - Sofia
5. Croatia - Zagreb
6. Czech Republic - Prague (Praha)
7. Denmark - Copenhagen (København)
8. Estonia - Tallinn
9. Finland - Helsinki (Helsingfors)
10. France - Paris
11. Germany - Berlin
12. Greece - Athens (Athena)
13. Hungary - Budapest (Budapest)
14. Iceland - Reykjavik
15. Ireland - Dublin
16. Italy - Rome (Roma)
17. Latvia - Riga
18. Lithuania - Vilnius
19. Luxembourg - Luxembourg City (Luxemburg)
20. Malta - Valletta
21. Netherlands - Amsterdam
22. North Macedonia - Skopje
23. Norway - Oslo
24. Poland - Warsaw (Warszawa)
25. Portugal - Lisbon (Lisboa)
26. Romania - Bucharest (București)
27. Russia (partly in Europe) - Moscow (Moskva)
28. Serbia - Belgrade (Beograd)
29. Slovakia - Bratislava
30. Slovenia - Ljubljana
31. Spain - Madrid
32. Sweden - Stockholm (Stockholm)
33. Switzerland - Bern
34. Turkey (European part) - Ankara
35. Ukraine - Kiev (Kyiv)

Some European countries do not have a defined capital city, such as the United Kingdom (England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland each have their own administrations but London is the seat of government for England), Vatican City (the Pope resides in the Vatican but it's not considered a country by many definitions), and Montenegro (Podgorica is considered the administrative center).
