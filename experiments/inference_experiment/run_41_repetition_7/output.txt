Artificial intelligence (AI) is a rapidly evolving field with significant implications for various industries and society as a whole. AI research is centered around specific goals and tools, such as reasoning, knowledge representation, natural language processing, and robotics.

The traditional long-term goal of general intelligence, the ability to perform any task at human level or better, remains an area of active investigation. Achieving this goal requires integration of various techniques, including advanced neural network architectures and methods from statistics, operations research, and economics.
