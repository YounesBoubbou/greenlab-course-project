Artificial intelligence has transformed industries by improving efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making processes across sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, finance, and logistics. In healthcare, AI aids in diagnostics and drug discovery. In manufacturing, it optimizes supply chains and production lines, reducing waste and increasing productivity. In finance, AI enhances risk assessment, fraud detection, and customer service through chatbots. Across all industries, AI drives innovation, enabling businesses to stay competitive and meet consumer demands more effectively.
