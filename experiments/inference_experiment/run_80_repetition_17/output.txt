**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **United Kingdom:** London
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Portugal:** Lisbon
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam
* **Belgium:** Brussels
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Luxembourg:** Luxembourg City
* **Ireland:** Dublin
* **Austria:** Vienna

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Bulgaria:** Sofia
* **Ukraine:** Kyiv
* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Moldova:** Chișinău


**Northern Europe:**

* **Sweden:** Stockholm
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Iceland:** Reykjavik

**Southern Europe:**

* **Greece:** Athens
* **Croatia:** Zagreb
* **Slovenia:** Ljubljana
* **Montenegro:** Podgorica
* **Serbia:** Belgrade

**Note:**

The definition of "Europe" can be debated, with some countries like Turkey and Cyprus considered transcontinental. This list reflects the generally accepted geographical boundaries of Europe.
