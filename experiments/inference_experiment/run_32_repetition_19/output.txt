10. Czech Republic - Prague (Praha)

43. Spain - Madrid
44. Sweden - Stockholm
45. Switzerland - Bern (Berna)
46. Ukraine - Kiev (Kyiv)
47. United Kingdom - London (England); Edinburgh (Scotland); Cardiff (Wales); Belfast (Northern Ireland)
