 Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving productivity, enhancing decision-making capabilities, and enabling new business models. In manufacturing, AI is used to optimize production lines and predict maintenance needs. In healthcare, it powers diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans. Retail businesses leverage AI for customer segmentation and targeted marketing. Financial services institutions use it for risk assessment and fraud detection. Essentially, AI has become a game-changer, streamlining processes, reducing errors, and driving innovation across various sectors.
