 Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving operational efficiency, productivity, and decision-making processes. AI's ability to analyze vast amounts of data quickly and accurately has revolutionized sectors such as healthcare, finance, and manufacturing. For instance, in healthcare, AI helps in early disease detection and personalized treatment plans. In finance, it aids in risk management and fraud detection. By automating routine tasks, AI frees up human resources for more complex problem-solving, fostering innovation and growth. Its potential is vast, with AI expected to contribute $15.7 trillion to the global economy by 2030.
