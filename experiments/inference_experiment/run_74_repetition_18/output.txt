## Capitals of European Countries:

**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris
* **United Kingdom:** London
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam
* **Belgium:** Brussels
* **Portugal:** Lisbon

**Central & Eastern Europe:**

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Slovenia:** Ljubljana
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Bulgaria:** Sofia

**Southern Europe:**

* **Italy:** Rome
* **Greece:** Athens
* **Turkey:** Ankara (not in Western Europe geographically, but culturally considered part of Europe)
* **Malta:** Valletta

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm
* **Iceland:** Reykjavik

**Other Territories:**

* **Monaco:** Monaco
* **San Marino:** San Marino
* **Vatican City:** Vatican City

**Note:** This list includes only recognized independent states in Europe. It does not include territories that are not states but are partially or fully under European control.
