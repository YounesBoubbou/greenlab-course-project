Here are the capitals of all European countries, as recognized by international organizations:

**Western Europe:**

* **Andorra:** Andorra la Vella
* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Belgium:** Brussels
* **France:** Paris
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **Ireland:** Dublin
* **Liechtenstein:** Vaduz
* **Luxembourg:** Luxembourg City
* **Monaco:** Monaco
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **United Kingdom:** London

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Bulgaria:** Sofia
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Moldova:** Chisinau
* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Russia:** Moscow (Note: Russia spans both Europe and Asia)
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Ukraine:** Kyiv

**Southern Europe:**

* **Albania:** Tirana
* **Bosnia and Herzegovina:** Sarajevo
* **Croatia:** Zagreb
* **Cyprus:** Nicosia
* **Greece:** Athens
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Kosovo:** Pristina (partially recognized)
* **Malta:** Valletta
* **Montenegro:** Podgorica
* **North Macedonia:** Skopje
* **Portugal:** Lisbon
* **San Marino:** San Marino
* **Serbia:** Belgrade
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Vatican City:** Vatican City

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Estonia:** Tallinn
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Iceland:** Reykjavik
* **Latvia:** Riga
* **Lithuania:** Vilnius
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm


Please note that the definition of "Europe" can be subject to some debate, particularly regarding countries like Russia and Turkey which straddle Europe and Asia.
