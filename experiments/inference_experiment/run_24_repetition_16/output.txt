 Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed industries by improving efficiency, productivity, and accuracy in various sectors. In manufacturing, AI is used for predictive maintenance, quality control, and automation of repetitive tasks. In healthcare, AI-powered tools assist in diagnosing diseases, analyzing medical images, and creating personalized treatment plans. Finance sector utilizes AI for fraud detection, risk assessment, and customer service. Retail industry benefits from AI through personalized recommendations and inventory management. Overall, AI has become a game-changer by streamlining operations, reducing costs, enhancing customer experiences, and driving innovation across industries.
