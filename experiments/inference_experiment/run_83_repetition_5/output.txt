This paper provides a comprehensive overview of artificial intelligence (AI), encompassing its definition, applications, historical development, and current impact.

Key insights include: AI's ability to perceive, learn, and act to achieve goals has led to transformative applications in diverse fields. While high-profile examples like self-driving cars and chatbots are widely recognized, many AI-powered tools have seamlessly integrated into everyday life, often going unnoticed. 

The field of AI has experienced periods of both rapid progress and setbacks, driven by advancements in techniques like deep learning and the transformer architecture.  However, the widespread adoption of AI also raises concerns about unintended consequences and ethical implications, necessitating discussions on responsible development and regulation.
