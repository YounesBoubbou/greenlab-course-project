 The article discusses how renewable energy sources like solar and wind power have grown due to technological advancements and decreasing costs. However, their intermittency poses challenges for energy systems. Solutions include developing energy storage technologies and integrating grids more effectively. Policymakers are also incentivizing the transition by offering tax credits and feed-in tariffs. Achieving carbon neutrality will require a holistic approach that includes energy efficiency, sustainable infrastructure development, and international collaboration.
