**Western Europe:**

* **France:** Paris

* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam

* **Portugal:** Lisbon

* **Poland:** Warsaw

* **Austria:** Vienna

**Southern Europe:**

* **Italy:** Rome
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Greece:** Athens

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Estonia:** Tallinn
* **Latvia:** Riga

* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Hungary:** Budapest

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm

**Additional Notes:**

* Monaco and Vatican City are not considered independent countries and do not have capitals.
* Gibraltar is a British Overseas Territory and does not have its own capital.
