However, it is important to note that Kosovo and Abkhazia are partially recognized countries whose capitals are Pristina and Sukhumi respectively, while not universally accepted as part of Europe by all international organizations. Additionally, the Isle of Man and the City of London are not technically capitals but territories or cities in their respective countries (UK).
