Not all European countries have their capital within their borders as some cities serve multiple capitals, and others share them. Here is a list of the official capitals of each country in Europe:

1. **Andorra** - Andorra la Vella
2. **Austria** - Vienna
3. **Belarus** - Minsk
4. **Belgium** - Brussels (official) / The three de facto capitals are Brussels, Antwerp, and Ghent.
5. **Bosnia and Herzegovina** - Sarajevo
6. **Bulgaria** - Sofia
7. **Croatia** - Zagreb
8. **Cyprus** - Nicosia (divided)
9. **Czech Republic** - Prague
10. **Denmark** - Copenhagen
11. **Estonia** - Tallinn
12. **Finland** - Helsinki
13. **France** - Paris
14. **Germany** - Berlin
15. **Greece** - Athens
16. **Hungary** - Budapest
17. **Iceland** - Reykjavik
18. **Ireland** - Dublin (official) / Three de facto capitals are Dublin, Belfast, and Cork.
19. **Italy** - Rome
20. **Kosovo** - Pristina
21. **Latvia** - Riga
22. **Lithuania** - Vilnius
23. **Luxembourg** - Luxembourg City (official) / The three de facto capitals are Luxembourg City, Esch-sur-Alzette, and Differdange.
24. **Malta** - Valletta
25. **Moldova** - Chișinău
26. **Monaco** - Monaco-Ville
27. **Montenegro** - Podgorica
28. **Netherlands** - Amsterdam (official) / The de facto capital is The Hague.
29. **North Macedonia** - Skopje
30. **Norway** - Oslo
31. **Poland** - Warsaw
32. **Portugal** - Lisbon
33. **Romania** - Bucharest
34. **Russia** - Moscow (official) / The de facto capital is also Moscow.
35. **San Marino** - City of San Marino and Domitius Rock
36. **Serbia** - Belgrade
37. **Slovakia** - Bratislava
38. **Slovenia** - Ljubljana
39. **Spain** - Madrid
40. **Sweden** - Stockholm
41. **Switzerland** - Bern (official) / The de facto capital is also Bern.
42. **Turkey** - Ankara (official) / The de facto capital was Istanbul until 1923, and the city continues to be economically and culturally significant.
43. **Ukraine** - Kyiv
44. **United Kingdom** - London (official) / Cardiff, Edinburgh, and Belfast are recognized as capitals of Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland respectively.

This list includes all European countries, their official or de facto capitals, and some notable points about how the concept of a capital is applied in certain cases.
