 Here is a list of the capitals for most European countries, although there may be some regional disputes or exceptions:

1. Austria - Vienna (Wien)
2. Belarus - Minsk
3. Belgium - Brussels
4. Bosnia and Herzegovina - Sarajevo
5. Bulgaria - Sofia
6. Croatia - Zagreb
7. Czech Republic - Prague (Praha)
8. Denmark - Copenhagen (København)
9. Estonia - Tallinn
10. Finland - Helsinki (Helsingfors)
11. France - Paris
12. Georgia - Tbilisi
13. Germany - Berlin
14. Greece - Athens
15. Hungary - Budapest
16. Iceland - Reykjavik
17. Ireland - Dublin
18. Italy - Rome (Roma)
19. Latvia - Riga
20. Lithuania - Vilnius
21. Luxembourg - City of Luxembourg (Luxembourg-Stadt or Luxembierg-Stad)
22. North Macedonia - Skopje
23. Malta - Valletta
24. Netherlands - Amsterdam
25. Norway - Oslo
26. Poland - Warsaw (Warszawa)
27. Portugal - Lisbon (Lisboa)
28. Romania - Bucharest (București)
29. Russia - Moscow (Moskva)
30. Slovakia - Bratislava
31. Slovenia - Ljubljana
32. Spain - Madrid (Madrid)
33. Sweden - Stockholm
34. Switzerland - Bern
35. Ukraine - Kyiv (Kiev)
36. United Kingdom - London (Londres in Welsh, Belfast is the capital of Northern Ireland)

Some regions such as Vatican City and Kosovo have their own capitals, but they are not part of Europe geographically. I hope this helps!
