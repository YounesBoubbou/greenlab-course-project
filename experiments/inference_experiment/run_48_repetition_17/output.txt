Artificial intelligence has transformed industries by improving efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making processes across sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, finance, and customer service. In healthcare, AI accelerates drug discovery and enhances diagnostic capabilities. In manufacturing, it optimizes supply chain management and predicts maintenance needs, reducing downtime. In finance, AI drives risk assessment, fraud detection, and personalized financial advice. Customer service has seen significant enhancements with chatbots providing 24/7 support, improving user experience and satisfaction.
