 Artificial intelligence has transformed industries by improving efficiency, productivity, and accuracy in various sectors. In manufacturing, AI is used for predictive maintenance and quality control. In healthcare, AI assists in diagnosis and treatment plans. In finance, AI powers risk assessment and fraud detection. Retail uses AI for personalized recommendations and inventory management. The common thread is the ability to analyze data at scale, learn from patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention. This not only saves time and resources but also enables new business models and revenue streams.
