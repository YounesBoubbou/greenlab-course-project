There are 27 member states of the European Union, each with its own capital city. The capitals of the European countries are:
1. Austria: Vienna
2. Belgium: Brussels
3. Bulgaria: Sofia
4. Croatia: Zagreb
5. Cyprus: Nicosia
6. Czech Republic: Prague
7. Denmark: Copenhagen
8. Estonia: Tallinn
9. Finland: Helsinki
10. France: Paris
11. Germany: Berlin
12. Greece: Athens
13. Hungary: Budapest
14. Ireland: Dublin
15. Italy: Rome
16. Latvia: Riga
17. Liechtenstein: Vaduz
18. Lithuania: Vilnius
19. Luxembourg: Luxembourg City
20. Malta: Valletta
21. Netherlands: Amsterdam
22. Poland: Warsaw
23. Portugal: Lisbon
24. Romania: Bucharest
25. Russia: Moscow
26. San Marino: San Marino
27. Slovakia: Bratislava
28. Slovenia: Ljubljana
It is important to note that some countries in Europe, such as the United Kingdom and Switzerland, are not part of the European Union and therefore do not have a capital city within its borders.
