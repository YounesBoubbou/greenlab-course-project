##  Capitals of European Countries:

Here's a list of the capitals of all widely recognized European countries, keeping in mind that there can be differing opinions on what constitutes Europe geographically and politically:

**Western Europe:**

* **Andorra:** Andorra la Vella
* **Austria:** Vienna
* **Belgium:** Brussels
* **France:** Paris
* **Germany:** Berlin
* **Ireland:** Dublin
* **Liechtenstein:** Vaduz
* **Luxembourg:** Luxembourg City
* **Monaco:** Monaco
* **Netherlands:** Amsterdam
* **Switzerland:** Bern
* **United Kingdom:** London

**Eastern Europe:**

* **Belarus:** Minsk
* **Bulgaria:** Sofia
* **Croatia:** Zagreb
* **Czech Republic:** Prague
* **Hungary:** Budapest
* **Moldova:** Chișinău
* **Poland:** Warsaw
* **Romania:** Bucharest
* **Russia (European part):** Moscow
* **Serbia:** Belgrade
* **Slovakia:** Bratislava
* **Ukraine:** Kyiv

**Northern Europe:**

* **Denmark:** Copenhagen
* **Estonia:** Tallinn
* **Finland:** Helsinki
* **Iceland:** Reykjavik
* **Latvia:** Riga
* **Lithuania:** Vilnius
* **Norway:** Oslo
* **Sweden:** Stockholm


**Southern Europe:**

* **Albania:** Tirana
* **Bosnia and Herzegovina:** Sarajevo
* **Greece:** Athens
* **Italy:** Rome
* **Kosovo:** Pristina
* **Macedonia (North Macedonia):** Skopje
* **Malta:** Valletta
* **Montenegro:** Podgorica
* **Portugal:** Lisbon
* **San Marino:** San Marino
* **Spain:** Madrid
* **Vatican City:** Vatican City 

Please note that this list reflects generally accepted capitals. There may be instances of contested territories or differing viewpoints on sovereignty, which could influence capital designations.
